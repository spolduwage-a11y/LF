About Lakshan Flora

We are dedicated to bringing nature's beauty to your doorstep. With years of experience, we craft floral experiences that leave a lasting impression.

Our Vision

To become the leading floral service provider in Sri Lanka, creating moments of joy through nature's beauty.

Our Mission

To deliver breathtaking floral arrangements using sustainable practices and providing exceptional customer service.

Our History

Founded in 2020 by Lakshan Alwis, we have grown from a small passion project to serving hundreds of happy customers.
